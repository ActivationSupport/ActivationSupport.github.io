Overview

How the Platform Fits Together

Two surfaces — a staff portal and a public customer-booking flow — each scoped and branded per office.

Staff Portal

A private, per-office dashboard for the activation team — order tracking, call lists, appointments, activation rates, churn, people & teams, training, and the daily report. Access is by private per-office link and PIN.

Customer Booking

A public per-office page where customers choose an activator and time. The confirmation email carries self-service reschedule and cancel links.
